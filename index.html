Designed to provide scalability, security, and user-friendly innovation, UXLINK ONE is a core component of UXLINKs Social Growth Layer, which integrates seamlessly with the One Account One Gas (OAOG) abstraction. Together, UXLINK ONE and OAOG protocol form the foundation of the UXLINK Social Growth Layer.
